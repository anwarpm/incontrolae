Intelligent Control for Wires and Cable Trading L.L.C.

At Intelligent Control for Wires & Cables Trading L.L.C., we specialize in delivering cutting-edge solutions in intelligent building technologies, along with high-quality electrical cables, wires, and low-voltage control systems.

With a strong and growing presence across the United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and Jordan, we cater to a broad spectrum of sectors including infrastructure, commercial, industrial, and residential developments throughout the Gulf and Levant regions.

Founded on the principles of technical excellence, reliability, and customer-centric service, our company draws on over a decade of industry experience through our affiliated companies and strategic partnerships. We understand the evolving demands of modern electrical and smart building systems and are committed to providing tailored, standards-compliant solutions that add value to every project.

At Intelligent Control, our goal is to be a trusted partner to consultants, contractors, and developers by offering dependable products, expert technical support, and efficient supply chain solutions. Our regional network ensures prompt service, on-time deliveries, and localized support—empowering our clients to complete their projects with confidence and precision.
